About Playing Padel:

Padel is a popular racquet sport, also known as padel tennis, played on a smaller court with glass walls and wire mesh. It is played in doubles using solid and perforated racquets and a slightly less pressurized ball. Padel offers fast-paced and dynamic gameplay, making it enjoyable for players of all ages and skill levels.
