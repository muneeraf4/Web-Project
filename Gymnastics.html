About Gymnastic:

Gymnastics is a sport that involves doing flips, jumps, and other cool moves. It requires strength, flexibility, and practice. There are different types of gymnastics, like artistic gymnastics where you do routines on different apparatus, and rhythmic gymnastics where you use props like ribbons and hoops. Gymnastics is fun and helps you stay fit.
